Guidelines

Follow these simple steps to manage your Shopify product images safely and effectively.

  1. Choose a store – Start by selecting the store you want to work with.
  2. Browse or search products – Use filters to quickly find what you need.
  3. Show Incompleted – Focus on products missing populated images.
  4. Populate Images – Write product variant images to metafields in seconds.
  5. Delete Asset Images – Clean up previously populated images if needed.
No customer data is read or stored. Only product and variant images are handled.
